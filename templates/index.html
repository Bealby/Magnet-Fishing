Magnet Fishing

Magnet Fishing is an exciting, growing sport, which involves 'casting' magnets into canals, lakes, off bridges and piers, in the hope to connect to any metal objects. 'Catches' can range from old nails and beer bottle tops; to bikes and even a scooter!

Magnet Fishing is an easy sport to take up. All you need is a strong magnet and rope and you’re ready to go! Such items can be purchased online, such as at First 4 Magnets, who provide international shipping.

This Website aims to bring fellow Magnet Fishers together and share their finds throughout the Stockholm area. ‘Catches’ can be input at Add Catches and are then shared at Catch Log. All catches have the option to be edited or deleted to make relevant changes.

So start your adventure in Magnet Fishing today.

Connect, share, and most of all, have fun!
