Magnet Fishing

Magnet Fishing is a niche, but growing sport, which involves 'casting' magnets into Canels, Lakes; and off Bridges and Piers; in the hope to connect to any metal artefacts. Artefacts that can range from Old Nails and Beer Bottle Tops; to Jewel and even a Scooter!

Magnet Fishing is an easy sport to take up. All you need is a strong magnet and rope and you’re ready to go! Such items can be purchased online, such as at First 4 Magnets, which provide international shipping.

This Website aims to bring fellow Magnet Fishers together and share their finds throughout the Stockholm area. ‘Catches’ can be inputted Add Catches and are then shared at Catch Log. All catches have the option to be edited or deleted to make relevant changes.

So start your adventure in Magnet Fishing today! Connect, share, and most of all, have fun!
